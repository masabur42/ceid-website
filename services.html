Our Services


Site Development


Civil Engineering & Innovative Design, LLC provides comprehensive site development services that transform project concepts into functional, sustainable, and buildable developments. We work closely with property owners, developers, architects, and public agencies to create efficient site layouts that maximize land use while meeting regulatory requirements. From initial feasibility studies through final construction plans, our team is committed to delivering practical, cost-effective solutions that support successful project outcomes.


Land Development


Our land development services guide projects from raw land to ready-to-build communities and commercial sites. Civil Engineering & Innovative Design, LLC specializes in planning, grading, drainage, stormwater management, utility planning, and permitting coordination. We focus on creating developments that balance environmental responsibility, economic value, and long-term functionality while helping clients navigate the complexities of local, state, and federal regulations.


Utility Design & Coordination


Reliable infrastructure is the foundation of every successful development. Our utility design and coordination services include water distribution, sanitary sewer, storm drainage, and utility relocation planning. Civil Engineering & Innovative Design, LLC collaborates with municipalities, utility providers, and project stakeholders to develop efficient utility systems that meet current standards, minimize conflicts, and support seamless project construction and operation.


Traffic Engineering


Civil Engineering & Innovative Design, LLC offers traffic engineering solutions that promote safe, efficient, and sustainable transportation systems. Our services include traffic impact studies, roadway access evaluations, intersection analysis, traffic control planning, and parking assessments. By combining technical expertise with practical design approaches, we help clients improve mobility, enhance public safety, and support successful project approvals.


Roadway Design


Our roadway design services deliver safe, durable, and efficient transportation infrastructure for communities and developments of all sizes. Civil Engineering & Innovative Design, LLC prepares roadway plans that incorporate geometric design, drainage, pavement design, signing and striping, and ADA-compliant features. We strive to create transportation networks that improve connectivity, accommodate future growth, and meet the requirements of local and state agencies.


Structural Engineering


Civil Engineering & Innovative Design, LLC provides structural engineering services focused on safety, durability, and innovative design. Our team develops structural solutions for commercial, residential, municipal, and infrastructure projects, ensuring each design meets applicable building codes and engineering standards. From structural analysis and foundation design to retaining walls and other support structures, we are dedicated to delivering dependable engineering solutions that enhance project performance and long-term value.
